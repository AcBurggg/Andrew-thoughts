Introduction to Computing Systems
Yale Patt & Sanjay Patel
Technical
Computer Organization
Rating: 5/5

Guides the reader step-by-step down the path from wires and silicon to fully working computers (albeit a very simple one called the LC-3).

James Kurose & Keith Ross
Technical
Networking
Rating: 3.5/5

Talks about each layer of the network stack in simple terms.

Rome

History

Stephen Greenblatt

Rome
Philosophy

Science

Describes the student-tutor relationship between two Enlightenment astronomers, one dedicated to the collection of unprecendentedly precise and accurate measurements (before the existence of telescopes!) and the other who used them to reshape our model of the solar system.
